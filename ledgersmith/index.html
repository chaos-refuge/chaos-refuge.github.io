LedgerSmith Support

LedgerSmith is a local-first personal finance app for macOS and iOS. It helps you track income and expenses, review financial insights, manage goals, and keep backups under your control.

Contact

For support, bug reports, or privacy questions, open an issue at GitHub Issues.

Please include your device model, OS version, LedgerSmith version, and a short description of the issue. Do not include sensitive financial details.
